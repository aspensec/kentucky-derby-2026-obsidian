## Running Style

Midrunner/Presser — The Puma is one of the most versatile pace types in the field. He rallied impressively to take the Tampa Bay Derby but also proved he could contest the lead in the Florida Derby. This ability to adapt makes him dangerous in any pace scenario — he can sit near the front if the speed collapses, or rate just off the pace and make a mid-race move. Trainer Delgado and jockey Castellano will have options on how to ride him.

## Notes

- At 10-1, one of the shorter-priced runners in the field and a legitimate contender.
- The combination of tactical versatility and graded stakes wins makes him a serious threat.
- Javier Castellano is a multiple Eclipse Award-winning jockey with tremendous big-race experience.
- Trainer Gustavo Delgado is also listed as a co-owner — an obvious personal investment in this horse's success.
- Watch his position at the first turn — where he settles early will be very telling about how Castellano plans to ride the race.
