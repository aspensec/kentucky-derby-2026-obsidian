## Running Style

Presser/Midrunner — Designated "E/P" (early pace/presser) in TwinSpires run style analysis, Great White sits somewhere between a frontrunner and a midpacker. He applies early pressure without necessarily leading, which gives him some versatility. He can adapt to a variety of pace scenarios and won't be left scrambling if the race develops differently than expected.

## Notes

- A late addition to the field, replacing the scratched Silent Tactic and Fulleffort — his inclusion adds intrigue as a horse that may not have been on everyone's radar early in Derby prep season.
- Trainer John Ennis is also listed as a co-owner alongside Three Chimneys Farm, giving him a personal stake in the outcome.
- Three Chimneys Farm is a respected Kentucky-based breeding and racing operation.
- Jockey Alex Achard will be looking to find a comfortable pressing position from the very wide Post 21 (actual gate 19).
- At 30-1, a longshot but his pace style can be effective if the race sets up right.
- Horse color not confirmed in available sources.
