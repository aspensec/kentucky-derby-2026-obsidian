## Running Style

Stalker/Midrunner — Danon Bourbon is a tactically flexible Japanese shipper who is eligible to race closer to the pace than many international runners. He sits comfortably in a mid-pack stalking position and tracks the leaders. His versatility is an asset — he won't be forced into one spot and can adapt to however the race sets up.

## Notes

- One of two Japanese Road qualifiers in the field, alongside Wonder Dean (Post 10).
- Japan has sent several high-profile runners to the Kentucky Derby in recent years with varying results.
- Trainer Manabu Ikezoe and jockey Atsuya Nishimura make the long trip to Churchill Downs as part of the Japanese contingent.
- The 1¼-mile distance and the chaotic start of a 20-horse field are the biggest question marks for any international runner.
- At 20-1, represents potential value if you believe international shippers are being underrated.
