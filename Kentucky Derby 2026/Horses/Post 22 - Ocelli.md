## Running Style

Backrunner/Deep Closer — Ocelli fits the profile of a deep closer who will be near the back of the pack for the majority of the race. He would need a complete pace meltdown — the kind where all the frontrunners completely fall apart in the final turn — to make a serious impact. His late run is his only weapon, and in a field this competitive, that one weapon needs everything to go exactly right.

## Notes

- A late addition to the field, replacing the scratched Fulleffort.
- **Notable historic footnote:** Ocelli is currently the only maiden in the field. If he wins, it would be the first maiden to capture the Kentucky Derby since Brokers Tip in 1933 — a gap of 93 years.
- Trainer D. Whitworth Beckman is not among the high-profile Derby regulars, making this a true underdog story.
- Jockey Joseph D. Ramos will have to be extremely patient and hope for a dream scenario to unfold ahead of him.
- Post 22 (actual gate 20) is the widest post in the field — but for a deep closer it hardly matters since he'll be last or near last regardless.
- At 50-1, tied with Intrepido as the longest shot on the board. The maiden angle is the primary reason to include him in any exotic wager.
