## Running Style

Presser/Midrunner — Designated "P" (Presser) in TwinSpires run style analysis, Litmus Test likes to run near the front without necessarily leading. He'll apply pressure to the frontrunners in the early and middle portions of the race, sitting comfortably in the 3rd–5th range, then try to hold on or kick clear in the stretch. This style is versatile and can work from a variety of post positions.

## Notes

- One of two Bob Baffert-trained runners in the field, alongside Potente (Post 14).
- Baffert's horses always attract attention given his track record; even at 30-1 this horse should not be overlooked.
- Martin Garcia is a capable jockey with experience at Churchill Downs.
- The presser/midrunner style may benefit from the expected hot pace — if the frontrunners tire, a presser can inherit the lead while still having reserves.
- The ownership group includes Stonestreet Stables, a high-profile thoroughbred operation.
