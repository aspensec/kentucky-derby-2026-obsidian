## Running Style

Closer — Albus is a late-running type who has historically been slow out of the gate, particularly in his 2-year-old campaign. He relies on picking up tired horses in the stretch rather than making any kind of early move. In a wide-open pace scenario that scenario can work, but in a 20-horse field he'll need a clean, uncrowded run through traffic late.

## Notes

- Long shot at 30-1; represents value if the pace collapses and sets up perfectly for closers.
- Trained by Riley Mott, who also has Incredibolt (Post 11) in the field — same barn, different running styles.
- Jockey Manny Franco will need to find a comfortable spot early and hope for a clear lane in the stretch.
- One of two Pin Oak Stud runners in the race alongside stablemate Incredibolt.
