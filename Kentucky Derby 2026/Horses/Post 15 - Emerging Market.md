## Running Style

Midrunner/Stalker — Emerging Market runs best from off the pace, but his connections are clearly concerned about him getting shuffled too far back in a large field. He looks to avoid getting caught in early traffic and will aim to sit in a comfortable mid-pack stalking position, with his best running coming in the final quarter mile. His style is about finding the sweet spot — not too close to a potentially suicidal pace, not so far back that he can't close the gap.
